Coffee at Kind

Met Varun and Tushar at Kind today. No agenda, no deck, no follow-up items. Just coffee and conversation.

There's something about a good café that makes thinking easier. Kind has that quality — unhurried, just the right amount of noise. The kind of place where an hour disappears without guilt.

We talked about the usual things that turn into the interesting things. What we're building, what we're reading, what's been sitting in the back of our heads. The conversation went where it wanted to.

Not every good thing needs to be productive. Sometimes a pleasant chat with good people over coffee is just that — and it's enough.
